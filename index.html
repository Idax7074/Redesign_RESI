What is special about La Fresca?

All the pasta and homemade sauces are made fresh, every day. La Fresca concept is well-known in Italy: You choose among several options of pasta and sauces and you bring it home to cook.

Easy as a supermarket trip, but good as at the restaurant!

Enjoy your delicious pasta!
